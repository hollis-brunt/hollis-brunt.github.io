Live Performance Manager - Event Producer - Curator

Hollis Brunt

Hollis Brunt is the Live Performance Manager at Misfit Music Management, where they specialize in touring and live strategies, including performance bookings, community engagement, and acting and speaking opportunities for artists. With a keen eye for detail and passion for live music, Hollis ensures every concert, tour, and engagement runs seamlessly, taking concepts to completion through negotiations, contracting, advancing, and tour management. This fall, they will be expanding Misfit’s artist services with a dedicated focus on building a dynamic roster of 2SLGBTQIA+ artists - continuing the company’s mission to champion underrepresented voices in music and create inclusive spaces across the live performance landscape in North America and abroad.
