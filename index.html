Hollis Brunt

Hollis Brunt is a Live Performance Manager, Event Producer, and cultural curator with over a decade of experience across film, television, theatre, and live events. Working internationally across North America and Australia, they create thoughtful, inclusive, and community-rooted experiences while advocating for 2SLGBTQIA+ artists at every stage of their career.

Hollis is currently building Misfit Live, with Misfit Music Management, a new agency launching in 2026 dedicated to expanding stages and opportunities for queer voices and their allies.
