Hollis Brunt

Hollis Brunt has spent over a decade working across film and television, theatre, and live events, becoming known for curating experiences that are thoughtful, unique, and inclusive. As an Event Producer, Hollis has worked internationally from North America to Australia, with a strong focus on community-rooted and family-friendly events that celebrate local creativity.

  • Advocating for emerging and established 2SLGBTQIA+ artists at every stage of their careers
  • Building meaningful connections with talent buyers and industry professionals across the globe

They are currently building Misfit Live, a new agency within Misfit Music Management, dedicated to expanding stages and spotlights for queer voices and their allies when it launches in 2026. Guided by collaboration and care, Hollis continues to shape culture and connection through live performance and artist development.
