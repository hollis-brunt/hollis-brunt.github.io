Curator | Live Performance Manager | Event Producer

Hollis Brunt

Hollis Brunt has spent over a decade working across many cultural sectors including film and television, theatre, and live events. Through their developed craft and passion for their work they have become known for curating experiences that are thoughtful, unique, and inclusive.

As an Event Producer, Hollis has worked internationally from North America to Australia. Their portfolio spans across multiple markets with a heavy focus on community-rooted events that celebrate local creativity in all its forms and family-friendly experiences that are fun for kids, parents, and everyone in between.

They bring that same energy, heart, and drive into music and live performance development by opening doors for emerging and established 2SLGBTQIA+ artists by advocating for artists at every step of their career. Hollis ensures their clients receive fair and respectful treatment across the industry, as well as connecting them with talent buyers and industry professionals across the globe to expand their voice.

They are currently building Misfit Live, a new agency within Misfit Music Management - a live performance and career-building arm rooted in championing 2SLGBTQIA+ artists across North America and beyond. Launching in 2026, Misfit Live is dedicated to expanding stages and spotlights for queer voices and their allies: from concerts and tours to speaking engagements, workshops, and on-screen opportunities.
